# A Case for Christ

Jesus Christ was falsely accused, unjustly tried, and condemned to death despite being innocent.

Even the Roman governor Pontius Pilate declared that he found no fault in Him, yet the crowd demanded His crucifixion.

Christ suffered humiliation, torture, and death on the cross.

But this was part of God's plan for the salvation of humanity.

The Bible teaches that Jesus was wounded for our transgressions and bore the punishment for our sins so that we may receive forgiveness and eternal life.

His sacrifice shows God's love and mercy toward mankind.

If you trust in the Lord Jesus Christ, your sins can be forgiven and you can receive the gift of salvation.
